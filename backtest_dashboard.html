Rules being tested

  • Universe: 98,457 unique modelled runner rows from 10,443 races; settled rows only are used for P/L.
  • Back shortlist guard: model odds and raw odds both shorter than real/bookmaker odds.
  • Each Way: decimal odds 13.0+ and at least 2 core support signals from model value, big EW, rank overlay, sleeper, raw odds or top-rated-clear.
  • Must Win: decimal odds 9.0 or shorter and clear top model score by 75+ points.
  • Must Lay: decimal odds ≤2.25, raw probability at least 20 pts weaker than market, and not model-ranked #1.
  • EW settlement: 1pt win + 1pt place, place leg paid at 1/5 odds using standard estimated place counts from field size. Historic extra-place offers are not assumed unless you add a historic extra-place feed.
